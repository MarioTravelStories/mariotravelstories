Yala Peak is a large mountain that is 5,7000m in height. This mountain is located in Langtang Region to the north of Kathmandu. The Nepal Mountaineering Association consider it as a trekking peak. The summit of Yala Peak gives a good view of Shishapangma which is 8,013m in height and also the highest mountain in Tibet.
